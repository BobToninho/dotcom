Now

November 28th, 2025

See nownownow.com for why this page exists. Thanks for the idea!

I am writing this /now page from my apartment in Vienna; outside it’s -2°C. I came back from a walk in the main shopping street of the city after a day of work. I often get inspired about a tweet saying “the best thing I did since I entered my apartment is staying out of it as much as possible”.

The past three week have been hectic. I flew in a different countries each week and it was rewarding. I saw my girlfriend in her erasmus place, friends and family.

Christmas is coming and the city doesn’t disappoint. I am not a big Christmas fan but the Christmas mood makes me calm, warm and happy. My idea of Christmas is a cozy lunch with my family with the fire and relaxed chats.
